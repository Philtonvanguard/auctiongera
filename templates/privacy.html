Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

Plain-English summary: We collect what you give us at signup (username, email, password) and what you do on the site (bids placed, items won/listed). We use it to run auctions, send required notices, and — only with your opt-in — marketing. We don't sell your data. Request deletion any time.

1. Who We Are

AuctionGera ("we," "us") operates this auction platform under the Vipelex brand. We are the data controller for personal information collected through this site.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Run the platform: authenticate logins, record bids, send win/outbid notifications, coordinate pickups.
  • Process payments: via integrated payment providers; we receive confirmation only, not card data.
  • Communicate: transactional emails (bid status, account security, won-item details). Required to operate the auction.
  • Marketing: only if you separately opt in. Unsubscribe any time without affecting transactional emails.
  • Security & fraud prevention: detect shill bidding, fake accounts, payment abuse.
  • Legal compliance: sales tax records, lawful requests, dispute records.

4. Legal Bases (GDPR)

If you are in the EU/EEA or UK, we rely on:

  • Contract: to deliver the auction platform service.
  • Consent: for marketing emails and any optional cookies.
  • Legitimate interest: security, fraud prevention, basic analytics.
  • Legal obligation: tax records, dispute records.

5. Who We Share Information With

We use a small number of trusted processors. We do not sell your data.

  • Hosting providers (Render, Railway, or equivalent) — site hosting and database
  • Payment processors — to handle transactions
  • n8n / webhook services — for internal automation of bid and payment events
  • Email providers — to send transactional and (opt-in only) marketing emails
  • Authorities — only when legally required
  • Counterparty — limited info (username, win confirmation, pickup coordination) shared between buyer and seller after a successful auction close

6. Cookies and Sessions

We use session cookies to keep you logged in, plus standard hosting/security cookies. We do not run cross-site advertising trackers.

7. How Long We Keep Data

  • Account and bid history: while your account is active, plus a reasonable period after closure.
  • Transaction records: up to 7 years (tax / accounting compliance).
  • Marketing list entries: until you unsubscribe, then removed within 30 days.
  • Server logs: typically 30–90 days.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (GDPR, UK GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, etc.) you may have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Receive a copy of your data in portable format
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority

Email contact@vipelex.com or use our Opt-Out page.

9. California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)

California residents have the right to know what categories of personal information we collect, the right to delete, the right to opt out of "sale" or "sharing" (we do not sell or share for cross-context behavioral advertising), and the right not to be discriminated against for exercising these rights.

10. Children

AuctionGera is for adults 18+. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has registered, contact us and we will delete the account.

11. Security

We use HTTPS everywhere, hashed passwords, encrypted backups, and reputable hosting/processing partners. No system is perfectly secure; if a breach affects you, we will notify you and the relevant authorities as required by law.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Material changes will be announced on this page with a new "Last updated" date and, where required, by email or in-app notice.

13. Contact

Privacy questions or rights requests: contact@vipelex.com.
